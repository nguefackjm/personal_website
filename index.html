Thank you for visiting this site!

"The greatest gift anyone can ever give you is information", We are sure someone already said that, what we are not sure of is when or by whom.

In that light, the purpose of this site is to showcase some passions of ours and the resources we use to pursue those passions.

Those passions featured here are mostly issues related to IT, Mathematics, Computer science\ engineering, Electrical engineering, writing or photography. The site is divided into four sections. Most sections are still under construction you are welcome to suggest topics for future exploration and to visit at a later date. The sections are home, story, gallery and external links. What follows is a brief summary of some of these sections:

Story

The gallery section contains the result of the author's adventures in photography. Its content will draw from the author's outdoor adventures.
